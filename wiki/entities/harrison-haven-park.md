# Harrison @ Haven Park Communities

Day-job context roster. Harrison is **Director of Asset Management at Haven Park Communities**, a PE-backed manufactured-housing operator on a portfolio growth path from ~30,000 units toward 60,000–80,000 units.

This page exists as a cross-reference target — other vault pages (especially Home Manager and anything that touches vendor-management-at-scale) link here to anchor "day-job context" without re-explaining it.

## Role in one line

Owner-side asset management of a scaling manufactured-housing portfolio: vendor networks, capex planning, operator oversight, acquisition integration.

## Why this matters to the vault

Several active projects are structurally downstream of the Haven Park muscle memory:

- **[[personal/projects/home-manager/index.md|Home Manager]]** — single-asset case of multifamily/portfolio vendor management. The retainer + broker-vs-W2 structure (see [[work/decisions/2026-04-20-home-manager-positioning]]) is a direct port of the property-management model Harrison runs professionally.
- **Vendor-playbook reuse** — contract templates, QA cadence, escalation paths all transfer to household scale with minor adaptation.
- **Portfolio-operator mindset** — the Home-Manager whitepaper ([[personal/projects/home-manager/whitepaper-claude-code-kit.md]]) applies portfolio-level thinking (cohort metrics, brokered-specialist networks, standardized operating procedures) to a single household.
