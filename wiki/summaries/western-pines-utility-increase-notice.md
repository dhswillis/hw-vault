# Western Pines Utility Increase Notice — Summary

> Template letter notifying residents of waste management rate increase from Texas Pride Disposal; effective November 1, 2025.

## Key findings

Letter from Western Pines management (October 1, 2025) announcing trash service rate increase to $31.02 per container per month, effective November 1, 2025. Cause: Texas Pride Disposal halted service pending new agreement with increased fees. Management explored alternatives, determined continuation with Texas Pride was "best option at this time." Letter includes apology for interruptions, commitment to seeking lowest possible rates, and contact info for questions (281-492-7171).

Template structure: salutation, thank you opening, problem statement (service interruptions), cause (hauler demand), exploration acknowledgment, decision rationale, rate announcement (effective date + amount), apology + reassurance, call to action (contact office). Professional, resident-friendly tone.

## Status / caveats

This is a template/letter. No financial details on prior rates, alternative bids evaluated, or timeline for further rate reviews. Effective date November 1, 2025 (document dated October 1, 2025 = 30-day notice). Suitable for mass mailing to residents or individual notices. Can be customized with resident names, addresses, specific management contact info.
