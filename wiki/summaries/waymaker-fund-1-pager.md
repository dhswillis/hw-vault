# Waymaker Stable Income Fund — Summary

Q1 2025 fund one-pager for Waymaker Capital Partners' Stable Income Fund. This is Harrison's work-domain document — the first real work content in the vault.

## Fund overview

Waymaker Stable Income Fund is a defensive lending fund that invests in loans backed by single-tenant retail properties 100% leased to quality tenants (Chick-fil-A, AutoZone, Starbucks, Whataburger, other NNN tenants).

## Key terms

- Target net return: 10-12% annually
- 8.0% preferred return paid monthly to investors
- Subordinated management fee (investors paid first)
- Three-year initial investment period before redemption
- Originations benefit from The Kase Group sales network (100K+ operator profiles, $700M avg annual transactions)
- Management team: $12B transaction experience including $5.2B in single-tenant NNN and retail across 80+ markets
