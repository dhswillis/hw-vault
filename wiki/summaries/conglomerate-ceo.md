# Willis Holdings Conglomerate CEO — Summary

> Autonomous AI conglomerate spec: one CEO agent (Opus), 11 divisions in two tiers (6 revenue + 5 corporate), Harrison as Chairman reviewing daily.

## Key findings

This is the highest-level vision document for the Willis Holdings autonomous system. One CEO agent runs everything autonomously between Harrison's daily reviews. Revenue divisions: Futures Trading (most mature), Arbitrage, Content, Real Estate, Business Development, Research. Corporate functions: Finance & Tax, Fundraising & Capital, HR & Bot Operations, IT & Infrastructure, Security & Data Integrity.

Decision framework: <$500 autonomous, $500–$5K execute + flag, >$5K propose + wait for approval. New business lines always proposed first. The Futures Trading division is the most mature — it runs Model 1 (QC: Sweep+IFVG) and Model 2 (backtester: BOS/sweep/VA/PGF), nightly batch optimization on VPS.

## Status / caveats

Vision document — most divisions are aspirational rather than operational. The Futures Trading division is the only one with real infrastructure. The content and real estate divisions reference the AI Trading Show and Haven Park respectively but lack implementation detail. See [[wiki/summaries/aio-system-blueprint|AIO Blueprint]] and [[wiki/summaries/unified-brain-architecture|Unified Brain]] for the current operational direction.
