Core Strategy: Portfolio of Non-Correlated Signal Models Tempo is NOT a single strategy. It's a portfolio approach — multiple independent signal models that generate non-correlated trade populations. Each model has its own entry logic, execution assumptions, and edge profile. The goal is 10R+ per day combined by blending models that win in different conditions.
