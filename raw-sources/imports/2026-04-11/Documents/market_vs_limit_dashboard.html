5. The Trade-Off

Market wins on raw pts/day. Limit Mid wins on risk-adjusted.

Best raw output: MARKET + No Lunch + Risk≤15 = +102.6 pts/day, 67% WR but DD is 39.8R.
Best risk-adjusted: LIMIT_MID + No Lunch + Gap≤4 = +62.2 pts/day, 57% WR, PF 6.14, DD 7.3R. Calmar ratio is 8.5x vs Market's 2.6x.
Best WR + PF: LIMIT_MID + No Lunch + Long + Risk≤20 + Gap≤5 = 66% WR, PF 11.80, DD 8.7R. 99 trades in 30 days (3.3/day).

The limit midpoint acts as a quality filter — it skips the runners that would've been coin flips and only fills on pullbacks where the trade has room to work. Combined with killing lunch, capping risk, and filtering gap size, you get a 66% win rate with an 11.8 profit factor. The trade count drops from 354 to 99, but every trade is high quality.
