## Triage

- **Safety first**: #7 (hot water heater vent cover) — combustion venting is not a defer-able item.
- **Root-cause before cosmetic**: #5 (kids' bath drywall/tile) — find the leak/moisture source before tearing out tile.
- **Inspection before scope**: #1 (roof) and #2 (master tub) — both need a pro's eyes before we can even write the scope.
- **Quick wins**: #3 (snake the master sink drain), #4 (toilet repair kit or swap), #6 (verify bath vent termination) — small-spend, high-relief items to knock out first.

## Suggested sequence

1. **Week 1** — Safety + quick wins: #7 (vent cover), #3 (drain), #6 (vent check), #4 (toilet).
2. **Week 2–3** — Inspections: #1 (roofer walk), #2 (plumber/remodeler on tub), #5 (find moisture source in kids' bath).
3. **Week 4+** — Larger projects staged based on inspection findings: kids' bath repair, roof work, master tub, front door replacement.
4. **Anytime** — #9 (fireplace/grill) once clarified.
